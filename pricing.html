Frequently Asked Questions

How does the credit system work?

Our credit system is designed to give you flexibility in how you use Yok-AI. Different actions cost a specific number of credits: text interactions cost 1 credit, image analysis costs 10 credits, audio transcription costs 5 credits per minute, and chart analysis costs 15 credits. Your monthly subscription includes a set number of credits that reset each billing cycle. Higher-tier plans allow unused credits to roll over to the next month.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the change takes effect immediately, and you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No credits are lost when upgrading, but unused credits may be forfeited when downgrading, depending on the rollover limits of your new plan.

What happens if I run out of credits?

If you run out of credits before your billing cycle renews, you have two options: wait until your next billing cycle when your credits refresh, or purchase additional credit packs. Credit packs start at 100 credits for $15 and are available in your account dashboard. These additional credits never expire and can be used at any time.

How secure is Yok-AI?

Yok-AI is built with security and privacy as core principles. All data is end-to-end encrypted, and we implement advanced anti-detection systems to ensure your assistant remains completely invisible during screen sharing and recordings. Your data is never stored or logged on our servers beyond the active session, and we maintain a strict no-log policy for all text, image, and audio inputs. Our stealth technology includes rendering protection, keystroke masking, and process obfuscation to maintain complete confidentiality.

What is the difference between the operating modes?

Yok-AI offers three distinct operating modes:

Ghost Mode: Available on all plans, this mode focuses on maximum privacy and minimal system footprint. It's optimized for basic intelligence needs while maintaining complete invisibility.

Insight Mode: Available on Shadow Executive and Phantom Intelligence plans, this mode provides enhanced analytical capabilities while maintaining strong privacy protections. It's ideal for deeper analysis and more complex queries.

Advantage Mode: Exclusive to the Phantom Intelligence plan, this is our most powerful mode with advanced processing capabilities, predictive insights, and competitive intelligence features.

Is there a refund policy?

Per our Refund Policy, all purchases are final and non-refundable except in limited circumstances. These exceptions include legal requirements, fraudulent transactions, and complete failure to deliver the service. Subscription plans can be canceled at any time to prevent future billing, but cancellation does not entitle you to a refund for the current billing period.
