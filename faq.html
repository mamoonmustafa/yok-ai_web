General Questions

What is Yok-AI?

Yok-AI is a private AI assistant designed for discrete use during meetings, calls, and work sessions. It provides real-time AI assistance through text, voice, and screenshot analysis while maintaining complete privacy and stealth operation.

How is Yok-AI different from other AI assistants?

Unlike other AI tools, Yok-AI is specifically designed for stealth operation - it's invisible to screen sharing, excluded from screenshots, hidden from taskbars, and includes advanced privacy protection features like keystroke blocking.

What can Yok-AI help me with?

Yok-AI can analyze meeting audio, provide real-time insights on screen content, answer questions during calls, help with technical issues, provide meeting summaries, and assist with any task that requires AI analysis - all while remaining completely hidden.

Pricing & Credits

How does the credit system work?

Each interaction with Yok-AI (text message, voice recording, or screenshot analysis) consumes credits. Voice recordings and screenshot analysis typically use more credits than simple text messages.

What happens when I run out of credits?

When your credits reach zero, you'll need to purchase additional credits to continue using Yok-AI. Your account remains active, but AI processing will be disabled until credits are added.

Do credits expire?

Yes, credits expire after 1 month. Yok-AI operates on a subscription-based model where you purchase monthly credit packages that must be used within the billing period.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Please refer to our refund policy at https://www.yok-ai.com/refundpolicy for complete details on refund terms and conditions.

Installation & Setup

What are the system requirements?

Yok-AI requires Windows 10 or Windows 11, .NET Framework 4.7.2 or higher, and an active internet connection. It works on both 32-bit and 64-bit systems.

How do I install Yok-AI?

After purchase, you'll receive a download link and license key. Simply download the installer, run it, and enter your email, password, and license key when prompted.

Does Yok-AI require administrator privileges?

No, Yok-AI runs with standard user privileges. However, some stealth features work better with elevated permissions.

Can I install it on multiple computers?

Each license is tied to a single user account. You can use it on different computers by logging in with your credentials, but concurrent usage may be limited.

Privacy & Security

Is my data private and secure?

Yes, Yok-AI is designed with privacy as the top priority. All communications are encrypted, and the application includes multiple stealth features to prevent detection.

Can others see Yok-AI during screen sharing?

No, Yok-AI uses advanced Windows APIs to exclude itself from screen captures and screen sharing applications like Zoom, Teams, and others.

Does Yok-AI store my conversations?

For detailed information about data storage and privacy practices, please refer to our privacy policy at https://www.yok-ai.com/privacypolicy

What about keystroke protection?

Yok-AI includes advanced keystroke protection (Ctrl+Alt+K) that prevents keyloggers from capturing your input when typing in Yok-AI's text fields.

Features & Functionality

What are the main features of Yok-AI?

Key features include:

  • Text-based AI chat
  • Voice recording and transcription (up to 30 seconds)
  • Screenshot analysis (up to 3 screenshots at once)
  • Stealth operation (invisible to screen sharing)
  • Global hotkeys for quick access
  • Meeting context and default prompts
  • Keystroke protection

Can I use Yok-AI during video calls?

Yes, that's exactly what it's designed for. Yok-AI remains completely hidden during video calls and screen sharing sessions.

How long can I record audio?

Voice recordings are limited to 30 seconds per message to ensure quick processing and reasonable credit usage.

How many screenshots can I analyze at once?

You can capture and analyze up to 3 screenshots simultaneously, which is useful for comparing different screens or getting comprehensive analysis.

When should I use voice vs. text vs. screenshots?

Use voice for quick questions during meetings, text for detailed queries, and screenshots when you need AI to analyze visual content like charts, code, or presentations.

Can I set custom prompts for different meeting types?

Yes, Yok-AI includes a context system where you can set default prompts for voice recordings and screenshot analysis based on your meeting type or use case.

Is there a limit to how much I can use Yok-AI?

Usage is only limited by your available credits. There are no daily or hourly usage restrictions.

What's the emergency exit feature?

Ctrl+Alt+X immediately closes Yok-AI and clears sensitive data, useful in situations where you need to quickly hide all activity.

What are the keyboard shortcuts?

Technical Support

What if Yok-AI doesn't work with my screen sharing software?

Yok-AI is tested with major platforms like Zoom, Teams, WebEx, and others. If you experience issues, contact support with details about your specific setup.

Can antivirus software interfere with Yok-AI?

Some antivirus programs may flag stealth applications. You may need to add Yok-AI to your antivirus whitelist for optimal performance.

What if I forget my login credentials?

Your Yok-AI login credentials are the same as your website dashboard login. If you need assistance with password recovery or account access, please contact our support team.

How do I update Yok-AI?

Updates are delivered through the application when available. You'll be notified when a new version is ready for download.

Troubleshooting

Yok-AI won't start or keeps crashing

Ensure you have .NET Framework 4.7.2 or higher installed, check your internet connection, and try running as administrator. Contact support if issues persist.

The hotkeys aren't working

Check if another application is using the same key combinations. You may need to close conflicting software or contact support for alternative hotkey options.

Voice recording isn't working

Ensure your microphone permissions are enabled and that no other application is exclusively using your audio devices.

Screenshots appear black or corrupted

This may occur with certain graphics drivers or display configurations. Contact support for assistance with display compatibility issues.

Account & Licensing

How long is my license valid?

License validity depends on your purchase option. Check your account details or contact support for specific information about your license duration.

Can I transfer my license to another person?

No, licenses cannot be transferred to another person. Each license is permanently tied to the original purchaser's account.

What if I change my email address?

Contact support to update your account email address. You'll need to provide your current license key for verification.

Do you offer bulk licenses for teams?

Contact support for information about team licenses and volume pricing options.

Still have questions? Contact our support team at support@yok-ai.com with your license key and detailed description of your question or issue.
